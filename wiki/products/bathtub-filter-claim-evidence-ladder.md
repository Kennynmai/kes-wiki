# Bathtub Filter Claim-Evidence Ladder

## Why this page exists
This page helps separate claim strength from evidence strength.

The bathtub-filter category repeatedly compresses many different proof levels into the same commercial impression. This ladder is meant to prevent that confusion inside KES research.

## Level 1 — strongest and narrowest
Examples:
- certified to reduce free available chlorine
- independently tested for chlorine reduction under stated flow conditions
- compatible with specific bathtub-spout formats

Use case:
- safest for technical and operational claims if documentation is real and specific

## Level 2 — usable with caution
Examples:
- third-party tested
- lab tested
- tested to standard X
- reduces chlorine smell

Use case:
- may be useful, but only if the test conditions and scope are clear

## Level 3 — explanatory, not proof of product efficacy
Examples:
- chlorine can contribute to dryness and irritation
- hard water may aggravate skin-barrier stress in some users
- some families with sensitive skin care about bath-water quality for understandable reasons

Use case:
- useful for educational framing and category relevance
- should not be transformed into direct finished-product efficacy promises

## Level 4 — anecdotal / conversion-heavy
Examples:
- helped my child’s eczema
- skin felt calmer after two baths
- no more flare-ups
- miracle for sensitive skin

Use case:
- useful for understanding commercial persuasion and user motivation
- weak as evidence for claim substantiation

## Level 5 — highest-risk wording
Examples:
- clinically proven to improve eczema-prone skin
- baby-safe because it removes harsh chemicals
- softens hard water in a clinically meaningful way
- removes a broad list of contaminants without route-specific validation

Use case:
- should trigger extra scrutiny, not automatic trust

## Key principle
The category’s biggest commercial temptation is to jump directly from:
- chlorine/hard-water concern is plausible

to
- therefore this bathtub filter improves eczema/sensitive skin

That jump is exactly where discipline is needed.
