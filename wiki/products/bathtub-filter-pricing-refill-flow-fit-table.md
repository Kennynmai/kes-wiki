## Main cross-brand pattern
The category appears to split into three economic models:
1. **short-cycle premium replacement** (Canopy, Santevia)
2. **longer-life bath-ball logic** (Crystal Quest)
3. **DTC “good enough + easy + family-safe” logic** (Tubo / Kinder style)

## Key caution
Capacity, months, and actual chlorine-reduction performance should not be assumed to align.

A product may look attractive on lifespan but underperform at realistic fill speed, or may perform well but impose a replacement cadence that hurts real-world value.
