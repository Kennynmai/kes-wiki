# 一、首页（Homepage）

### 区块 1 — Hero
- **Eyebrow**：`Filter media you can see. A formula you can read.`（CN：看得见的滤料，读得懂的配方。）
- **H1**：`You read your food labels. You read your skincare. What about your bath water?`
- **Sub**：`A clear cartridge — the media, the amount, the order, all visible. Clean is what you can see.`
- **CTA 主**：`Find your water →`　**CTA 次**：`See what's inside`
- *护栏*：不放未坐实数字；「可见」不暗示「更有效」。

### 区块 2 — The Reframe
> `You've always read the label.`
> `The back of the food. The actives in the serum — if you can't read it, you put it back.`
> `But there's one thing you use every day and never turn around: your bath water.`

### 区块 3 — The Villain（黑箱对照）
- 左（不透明竞品）：`Most bath filters: a handful of beads you can't see, a few claims you can't check.`
- 右（KES 透明盒）：`KES: turn it around. Look inside.`
- 一句：`They're betting you won't look. We made the box clear.`
- *护栏*：villain = 不透明，**不得**出现脏水/毒水画面。

### 区块 4 — See Inside（成分美学）
- `This is KDF55 copper-zinc. This is calcium sulfite. No third thing you can't name.`
- 逐层：`① PP fiber catches particles → ② KDF55 copper-zinc → ③ calcium sulfite reduces free chlorine`（水流向标注）
- `No cheap filler hiding behind opaque plastic.`
- *护栏*：metal-first 仅料级叙事，非去重金属健康。

### 区块 5 — Test It Yourself
- `Don't take our word for it. Test it yourself.`
- `Every filter ships with a free-chlorine test strip. Compare your water before and after.`
- `Independently tested free-chlorine reduction at stated fill flow: [去氯率·待第三方实测].`
- *护栏*：用游离氯试纸**非 TDS 笔**；数字未坐实前用占位。

### 区块 6 — Match Your Water（选择器）
- 标题：`Your water shouldn't use someone else's cartridge.`
- `Enter your ZIP — we'll tell you if your city runs free chlorine or chloramine, and which media stack fits.`
- 三卡：`Free-chlorine city` / `Chloramine city` / `Private well`
- 诚实劝退：`Chloramine city? V1 isn't for your water yet — leave your email and we'll tell you when the chloramine version ships.`

### 区块 7 — What We Won't Say（disclaim 带）
> `We won't tell you it "treats" anything.`
> `It doesn't soften hard water. It isn't a medical device. It doesn't remove heavy metals as a health benefit.`
> `It does one thing, and shows you the proof: it reduces free chlorine.`
- *承重句*：第一行不可删。

### 区块 8 — Real Proof
- `Real reviews — the good and the bad.`
- `KDF55 media inherits an NSF/ANSI 42 material-level listing from our supplier. The finished product is not itself NSF-certified — verify with the included strip.`

### 区块 9 — Final CTA
- `Filter media you can see. A formula you can read.`
- **CTA 主**：`Find your water` / **CTA 次**：`Read why we made the box clear →`（→ About）

# 二、产品页（PDP）

### 标题区
- **Product title**：`KES Bath Filter — Free-Chlorine, Clear Cartridge`
- **One-liner**：`A bath-fill filter that reduces free chlorine — with the media visible and the result you can test yourself.`

### Key facts（要点卡）
- `Reduces free chlorine` （**不是** total chlorine / chloramine）
- `Clear cartridge — see the real media and amount`
- `Includes a free-chlorine test strip`
- `Fits standard US tub spouts (18–25 L/min); no-overflow envelope up to 35 L/min`
- *护栏*：每条兼容声明都带「不支持」边界。

### What's inside（媒体透明）
`Layered, never mixed: PP fiber disc → KDF55 (110 g) → calcium sulfite (130 g), with an internal flow-diversion module. Each layer is replaceable separately. KDF55 is the visible safety/biofilm-stability layer; calcium sulfite is the primary dechlorination workhorse.`

### How it works（防沟槽叙事）
`Unlike mixed-bead products, KES uses strict layered media with a flow-diversion module — so water passes through each layer in order instead of channeling around it. That's why we can show you the layers and let you replace them one at a time.`
- *护栏*：解释技术选择，非贬竞品；不点名 ASIN。

### Performance & testing（如实口径）
`Free-chlorine reduction is strongest in the fresh-filter, best-experience segment and at lower fill flow (stated at 15 L/min). Independently tested figure: [去氯率·待第三方 DPD 实测]. Reduction follows a posted curve over filter life — replace at the marked trigger. The finished product is not NSF-certified; KDF55 media inherits an NSF/ANSI 42 material-level listing from the supplier. Verify your own water with the included chlorine test strip — not a TDS pen (this product does not target TDS).`
- *承重句*："The finished product is not NSF-certified."

### Replacement & verification
`Replace based on baths, not months. At 2 ppm tap chlorine, ~3 baths/week: soft trigger ≈ 113 baths; replace by ≈ 143 baths (~1 year). Lower tap chlorine ≈ longer life. Your local tap chlorine affects lifespan — verify with the strip.`
- *护栏*：baths/gallons 不用 months。

### FAQ（技术级，照 register）
- **Does it soften hard water?** `No. It reduces free chlorine. It does not soften your water. For scale on your tub/fixtures, add the anti-scale module — but that protects fixtures, it doesn't soften water either.`
- **Does it remove chloramine?** `Not this version. If your city uses chloramine, the free-chlorine version won't help — the chloramine version (catalytic carbon + a soak step) is coming. Enter your ZIP to check your city.`
- **Is it safe for babies / good for eczema?** `We don't make health or skin claims. Many families use a bath filter for chlorine-sensitive bathtime — but we won't tell you it treats anything.`
- **Does it remove heavy metals?** `We don't claim that as a health benefit. Our copper-zinc is a premium media grade you can see — not a heavy-metal health claim.`
- **How do I know it's working?** `Test it yourself with the included free-chlorine strip — before and after. Not a TDS pen.`
