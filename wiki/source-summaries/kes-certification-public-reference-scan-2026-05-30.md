# Source Summary — KES Certification Public Reference Scan (2026-05-30)

## Why this source matters
The internal source-table readout says which certification families KES currently has. This public-reference scan explains what each certification family means, where it should be verified, and what the evidence does or does not support.

## Extracted facts / observations
- IAPMO R&T publishes a Product Listing Directory for current listing verification, and IAPMO certificate PDFs themselves warn that the certificate document alone is not evidence of current listing status.
- ASME A112.18.1/CSA B125.1 is the plumbing supply fittings standard family behind many cUPC plumbing-product listings.
- NSF/ANSI/CAN 61 evaluates health effects of materials, components, products, and systems in contact with drinking water. It is not a flow-efficiency label and not a contaminant-reduction performance claim.
- NSF/ANSI/CAN 372 is a lead-content / lead-free standard for drinking-water-system components. It should not be treated as a lead-removal claim.
- DOE faucet and showerhead compliance is a regulatory certification / compliance-reporting surface under 10 CFR Part 429 and Part 430, with product-specific public fields such as maximum water use and pressure.
- California Title 20 requires regulated appliance performance to be certified to CEC; MAEDbS is the public database surface.
- EPA WaterSense applies to specific product categories, including lavatory faucets and showerheads. WaterSense is independently certified and tied to water efficiency plus performance criteria, not general product quality.
- CE and UKCA require care: official guidance treats CE/UKCA as conformity marking regimes tied to applicable product legislation and declarations. If no applicable EU requirements exist for a product, CE marking must not be used.
- ISO 9001:2015 is a company quality-management-system standard. It is not a product certificate and does not certify individual SKU safety, performance, or regulatory compliance.

## Caveats / ambiguity
- Public web search surfaced one KES IAPMO NSF/ANSI/CAN 61 certificate PDF hosted on Amazon media, but it is historical and marked void after November 2024; the IAPMO directory remains the current-status ground truth.
- Public search did not establish current KES-specific public listings for all certification families. Current KES status in the maintained register therefore relies on the internal 2026-05-30 source-table readout until each external listing is checked.
- CE/UKCA pages need internal certificate PDFs and test reports before they can be treated as complete evidence.
