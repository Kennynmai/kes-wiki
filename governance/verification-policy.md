# Verification Policy — KES Company Wiki

The agent can draft, synthesize, and organize.
Humans own verification, approval, and accountability.

## Verification levels
- `unverified`
- `spot-checked`
- `verified`

## High-stakes pages
Require stronger verification expectations:
- decision
- market
- strategic synthesis
- compliance-sensitive platform pages
- policy pages
- metric definition pages
- process-critical playbooks
- major risk pages

## Rule
The higher the consequence of being wrong, the stronger the verification requirement.
