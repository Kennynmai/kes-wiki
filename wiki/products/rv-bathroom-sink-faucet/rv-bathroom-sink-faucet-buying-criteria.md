# RV Bathroom Sink Faucet Buying Criteria

## Why this page exists
This page converts review and category research into the criteria a buyer actually uses to choose an RV bathroom faucet.

It should guide:
- product definition
- Amazon image stack
- bullet copy
- comparison tables
- test proof priorities

## One-line buyer job
The buyer wants to replace a cheap, old, leaking, short, or ugly RV bathroom faucet with something that fits the existing holes, installs quickly, looks better, and does not leak.

## Purchase Criteria Ranking
| Rank | Buying criterion | Buyer language | What KES must prove |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | Direct replacement fit | `fit perfectly`, `4-inch`, `RV sink`, `no modification` | 2-hole / 4-inch centerset, hole size, deck thickness, shank length, thread spec |
| 2 | No leak / no drip | `does not drip`, `no leaks`, `won't flood cabinet` | shutoff test, pressure test, internal body leak test, swivel leak test |
| 3 | Easy DIY install | `5 minute install`, `no plumber`, `quick replacement` | parts included, underside dimensions, clear install steps, no hidden adapters |
| 4 | Better material / less cheap | `not plastic`, `actual metal`, `not flimsy` | metal parts map, material callouts, torque proof |
| 5 | Better hand clearance | `can get my hands under it`, `high rise`, `gooseneck` | spout height, spout reach, water landing point |
| 6 | Finish / visual upgrade | `looks nice`, `modern`, `matches decor` | matte black / brushed nickel finish, water spot / wipe-down proof |
| 7 | Price still sensible | `worth the money`, `good price` | justify premium by reducing failure risk and install uncertainty |
| 8 | Trust / warranty | `replacement`, `customer service`, `return window` | early leak replacement path, warranty clarity |

## What Buyers Are Not Primarily Buying
- Luxury bathroom design.
- Complex faucet technology.
- Water filtration or health claims.
- A heavy residential faucet unless it clearly fits RV conditions.

## Bullet Copy Architecture
1. `Direct RV replacement` — fit, hole spacing, deck mount.
2. `Metal where RV faucets usually fail` — metal-critical structure.
3. `Leak-tested shutoff` — after actual proof.
4. `High arc with measured reach` — not just style language.
5. `DIY install kit and support` — parts and dimensions.

## Words To Use
- direct replacement
- 4-inch centerset
- 2-hole
- RV bathroom sink
- camper / travel trailer / motorhome
- metal threaded connections
- ceramic cartridges
- extended shanks
- measured spout reach
- leak-tested

## Words To Avoid
- universal fit
- never leaks
- lifetime drip-free
- heavy duty household faucet
- all metal, unless literally true
- lead-free / cUPC / NSF / WaterSense unless SKU evidence is complete

## Current KES Positioning Draft
> A metal-first RV bathroom faucet built for direct 4-inch replacement: stronger threaded connections, ceramic cartridges, measured reach, and clearer fit dimensions for small RV sinks.
