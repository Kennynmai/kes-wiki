# Japan

## Opportunity thesis
Japan is a target expansion market that should be evaluated with explicit market-entry assumptions, platform fit analysis, and product/category suitability review.

## Constraints / requirements
- market-entry requirements need dedicated evidence gathering
- platform-specific constraints must be separated from market-wide assumptions
- product localization, compliance, and demand assumptions require validation

## Open questions
- which product lines have the best initial fit?
- what are the most material compliance and localization barriers?
- should KES enter through one channel first or evaluate multiple in parallel?

## Risks
- conflating market attractiveness with operational readiness
- missing hidden compliance or localization requirements
- entering too broadly before proving product-channel fit

## Next research steps
- build source summaries for Japan market materials
- compare Amazon JP requirements with current KES capabilities
- identify product categories most suitable for initial entry
