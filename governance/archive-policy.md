# Archive Policy — KES Company Wiki

Archive pages when they are no longer part of the active working surface but still have historical or referential value.

## Common archive cases
- completed and no longer active
- superseded by newer decisions or playbooks
- no longer operationally relevant
- retained mainly for historical context

## Archive handling rules
When archiving a page:
1. set `status: archived`
2. update `updated`
3. note why it was archived
4. link to the superseding page when relevant
5. remove it from active dashboards when appropriate

## Rule of thumb
Archive when a page is worth keeping but no longer worth leading with.
