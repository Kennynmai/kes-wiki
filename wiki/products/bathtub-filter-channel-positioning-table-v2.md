# Bathtub Filter Channel Positioning Table — V2

## Version links
- Earlier page: [[bathtub-filter-channel-positioning-table]]

| Channel identity | Representative brands/surfaces | Core promise | Quote anchor | Strength | Weakness |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Premium baby/wellness DTC | Canopy, Motherly, Babylist | gentler, safer, more comfortable bath time for babies/families | “baby bath gear that’s worth the $89 price tag” | high emotional conversion; design premium | performance and claim scrutiny rise with price and softness framing |
| Premium wellness / lifestyle DTC | Santevia, mindbodygreen-style context | cleaner bath ritual, skin comfort, mineral/wellness story | Santevia product/support pages emphasize 100 baths / 2 months; Canopy/mindbodygreen emphasize barrier comfort and spa-like bath | editorial + product storytelling fit | can blur comfort story into quasi-clinical promise |
| Legacy filtration / utility | Sprite, Home Depot-adjacent logic | chlorine-focused filtration with replaceable cartridges | “Chlorgon filtration media helps to remove free chlorine, dirt, and odors” | narrower, more technically believable | less emotionally resonant / less premium visual appeal |
| Marketplace mass | Amazon, Walmart, generic lookalikes | broad contaminant list + easy install + family-safe | repeated marketplace snippets: “2,500 gallons”, “hard water softener”, “remove contaminants” | breadth and velocity | genericization, overclaiming, weak trust |
| Reviewer / comparator layer | WaterFilterGuru, YouTube review shorts | objective testing, worth-it framing, performance vs price | “unable to effectively remove chlorine at a ‘normal’ faster faucet flow rate” | shapes belief and skepticism | methods and incentives still need interpretation |
| Sensitive-skin / eczema DTC | Kinder, Tubo, social parent hooks | eczema-friendly / improve skin hydration / safer for kids | “the bath filter every parent needs” / “clinically tested to improve skin hydration” | strongest emotional urgency | highest claim/compliance risk |
