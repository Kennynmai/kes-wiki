# Page Types — KES Company Wiki

Primary canonical knowledge page types:
- `platform`
- `market`
- `product`
- `category`
- `project`
- `decision`
- `playbook`
- `synthesis`

Supporting governance/page types:
- `source-summary`
- `risk`
- `metric`
- `meeting-summary`
- `policy`
- `assumption`
- `glossary`

## Design rules
1. Each page type must have a distinct business role.
2. Facts, judgments, decisions, and procedures must stay separable.
3. Prefer canonical pages over fragmented duplicates.
4. Durable pages beat ad hoc pages.
5. Structure should improve as the company grows, not degrade.
