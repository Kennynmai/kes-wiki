# RV Bathroom Sink Faucet 下一步执行计划 — V1

## 这页的目的
这页把当前 research package 转成实际可执行的 next-step plan。

它回答的不是“这个品类有没有意思”，而是：

> 如果 KES 还要继续，这件事下一步到底怎么做？

## 当前总原则
当前建议不是 launch，而是：
- 结束继续扩写型 desk research
- 进入 sample acquisition + teardown + validation testing
- 在测试后再做一次明确 continue / pause / archive decision

## Workstream 1 — concept narrowing
### 目标
只保留：
- 1 个 lead concept
- 1 个 backup concept

### 当前建议
- Lead: metal-critical hybrid route
- Backup: full metal / brass-heavy premium route
- Archive: commodity ABS clone

### 输出物
- Lead concept one-pager
- Backup concept one-pager
- Rejection note for ABS clone

## Workstream 2 — competitor / sample acquisition
### 目标
拿到足够诚实的 benchmark samples，避免只靠页面判断。

### 建议样品结构
- 2 个 Amazon ABS 跑量款
- 1 个 premium ABS high-arc / swivel 款
- 1 个 OYMOV 或同类 actual-metal / hybrid RV 款
- 1 个 Empire / Dura / Valterra 等 RV parts channel 款
- 1 个普通 residential 4-inch centerset metal faucet
- 1 个 KES 现有 bathroom faucet 作为内部对照

### 输出物
- sample shortlist
- priority buy list
- teardown table
- per-sample risk notes

Starting point: [[rv-bathroom-sink-faucet-sample-shortlist-and-pricing]]

## Workstream 3 — teardown
### 目标
把页面语言和真实结构对上。

### 必看项
- body material
- waterway material
- shank length
- thread material and specification
- cartridge type
- handle/stem connection
- swivel joint design
- finish quality
- weight
- included parts

### 输出物
- teardown photos
- dimension table
- failure-point map
- KES design implications

## Workstream 4 — validation test setup
### 目标
把 [[rv-bathroom-sink-faucet-test-gating-checklist-for-kes]] 变成真实可运行的内部测试。

### 必做模块
- shutoff / drip
- pressure / leak
- thread / shank torque
- RV vanity fit bench
- spout reach and splash
- swivel leak
- thermal cycling
- finish durability

### 输出物
- test sheet template
- pass/fail thresholds
- equipment / fixture list
- sample test results

## Workstream 5 — compliance and claim guardrails
### 目标
防止页面先写，证据后补。

### 输出物
- allowed claims
- conditional claims
- banned claims
- certification evidence cards
- SKU mapping status

Starting point: [[rv-bathroom-sink-faucet-claim-register]]

## Workstream 6 — commercial model
### 目标
验证 metal-first 是否能商业成立。

### 必做项
- landed cost
- FBA fee
- warranty reserve
- price sensitivity assumption
- margin by route
- launch SKU count

### 输出物
- price band recommendation
- margin model
- V1 SKU recommendation
