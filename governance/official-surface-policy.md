# Official Surface Policy — KES Company Wiki

The repository has at least two conceptual layers:

## Working surface
Exploration, synthesis, draft reasoning, assumptions, and in-progress analysis.

## Official surface
Stable, reviewed, and organization-relevant guidance.

## Typical official surfaces
- reviewed or official playbooks
- official decision pages
- reviewed/official canonical platform pages
- reviewed/official canonical market pages where appropriate
- approved policy pages
- trusted onboarding dashboards
- reviewed metric definition pages

## Typical working surfaces
- source-summary
- meeting-summary
- assumption
- exploratory synthesis
- draft risk
- in-progress project analysis

## Dashboard rule
Maintain a clear entry point for broad company use, such as `dashboards/official-start-here.md`.
