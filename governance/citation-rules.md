# Citation Rules — KES Company Wiki

Important claims must remain traceable.

## Core rule
High-value pages should include a `Sources` section.
Especially:
- decision
- synthesis
- market
- platform
- risk
- policy
- metric

## Minimum citation behavior
Pages should usually link to:
- relevant `source-summary` pages
- relevant maintained pages they build on
- direct raw source references when needed

## Agent discipline
1. do not detach strong claims from sources
2. prefer traceable statements over elegant unsupported synthesis
3. update `Sources`
4. surface uncertainty when provenance is weak
