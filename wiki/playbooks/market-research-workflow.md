# Market Research Workflow

## Trigger / scope
Use this workflow when evaluating a new market, category expansion, or platform-market combination.

## Inputs
- source materials in inbox/raw
- current canonical pages
- known assumptions and open questions

## Steps
1. capture sources into inbox
2. classify sources by type
3. narrow reading using context-budget discipline
4. create source-summary pages
5. update canonical market/platform/product/project pages
6. identify risks and assumptions
7. produce a synthesis page if the analysis becomes reusable
8. route high-stakes conclusions for review

## Quality checks
- facts, judgments, decisions, and procedures remain separated
- important claims remain traceable
- canonical pages updated instead of duplicating content elsewhere

## Exceptions
- one-off exploratory conversations do not need full workflow treatment
- weakly grounded material should stay draft-level

## Failure modes
- reading full sources too early
- skipping source classification
- producing answers without wiki updates
- mixing exploratory conclusions into official surfaces too early
