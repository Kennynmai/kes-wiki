# RV Bathroom Sink Faucet 样品短名单与价格假设

## Why this page exists
This page turns the research into a buy / teardown shortlist.

The purpose is not to find the single best market product. The purpose is to buy enough samples to answer KES's key questions:
- What actually fails in ABS RV faucets?
- What does "metal" mean in existing RV listings?
- How much does real metal change cost, weight, installability, and perceived value?
- What dimensions do credible RV pages disclose?

## Test Questions Per Sample
1. Does the product still look trustworthy after handling?
2. Are threads plastic, metal, or metal insert?
3. Is shank length enough for thicker RV vanity tops?
4. Can supply lines be locked without over-torque?
5. Does the faucet drip after cycling?
6. Does the swivel joint leak?
7. Does the spout reach land water in the basin center?
8. Does the listing's material language match teardown reality?

## KES Price Hypothesis
### Conservative target
`$34.99-$39.99`

Best if KES chooses metal-critical hybrid and can keep BOM controlled.

### Stronger premium target
`$44.99-$49.99`

Only defensible if the product includes:
- visible metal-critical architecture
- extended shanks / better install kit
- leak testing
- SKU-level compliance evidence
- clearer warranty

### Avoid
Below `$29.99` if it forces KES back into commodity ABS tradeoffs.

Above `$59.99` unless full metal tests and user proof show clear willingness-to-pay.
