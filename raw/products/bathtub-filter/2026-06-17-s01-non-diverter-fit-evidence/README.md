## User-Provided Test Result

- Taxonomy: S-01 straight non-diverter, stable.
- Fit boundary: terminal bend circumference <= 18 cm is compatible.
- Common observed range: 15-17 cm circumference at the terminal bend.
- Accessory logic: included 5-hole silicone tie, 124 mm total length and 20 mm width, plus included 3M adhesive hook can simulate a diverter hanging point.
- Retention logic: the primary retention force is the silicone tie's tension and friction. The 3M adhesive is an auxiliary anti-slip layer to prevent the hard ABS hook from sliding against the metal tub spout contact surface; it is not the primary load-bearing mechanism.
- Silicone tie size: 124 mm total length, 20 mm width.
- Stretch limit noted by user: the 124 mm silicone tie can stretch to 22 cm circumference.
- Fit result: user states this S-01 setup has been physically tested and can be used within the above circumference boundary.

## Evidence Boundary

This evidence supports S-01 non-diverter spouts where the terminal bend circumference is <= 18 cm and the spout surface allows the 5-hole silicone tie, 124 mm total length and 20 mm width, plus 3M adhesive hook to form a stable hanging point.

Do not generalize this into:

- all non-diverter spouts are supported
- all square / rectangular / decorative spouts are supported
- all surfaces are safe for long-term silicone-tie friction and hook stability
- dynamic fill behavior has been fully validated

Still missing from this evidence item:

- exact measured circumference for each pictured spout
- outlet center-to-wall distance
- strap hole position used
- hook placement photo after installation
- hook displacement / rotation after tightening the silicone tie
- flow rate during test
- leak / overflow / bypass observations
- repeated fill-cycle stability
