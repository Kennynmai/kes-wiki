# Write-Back Policy — KES Company Wiki

Every substantive task should be evaluated for two outputs:
1. the immediate deliverable
2. relevant updates to maintained company knowledge

## Write back when
- reusable
- repeated
- structural
- operational
- decision-relevant
- policy / process relevant
- onboarding-relevant

## Do not write back by default when
- trivial
- disposable
- weakly grounded
- duplicative
- purely presentational
- explicitly excluded

Do not write weakly grounded content into high-trust surfaces as if it were official.
